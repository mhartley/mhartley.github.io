Senate District 24

Hello, Denver Friends

Below are services that I think an independent organization should provide in support of a race (primary or otherwise) in SD24. I've listed them here, in order of importance.

Per Audrey's request, they're stacked to accomodate scale. On the smallest budget, we might only have cash for the first priority, with a little flexibility to scale ads. As the budget grows, we would have room to incorporate more of these services.

Email Collection: Established Demographics

Serve targeted ads to established primary voters within the district. The goal is to foster engagement, raise name id, and collect hard confirms of support through click throughs. We will also focus on using these ads to build an email list that can be used to solicit volunteers and donations. The list itself can be donated to candidates if we choose.

Ideally, we would serve ads to democratic voters on FEC and SOS lists that matched a digital profile and lived in or nearby the district.

Demographic ROI profiling

Using a proprietary regression algorithm, we'll iterate through combinations of a list of demographic variables that exist as targetable options on Facebook and other social media services. Little by little, the ads are focused only on targets that have the highest return on investment in terms of cost/user engagement or cost/email. At the end, we not only have emails, but a matrix of expected ROI for ads on a variety of demographics. This valuable data can be shared with campaigns and other supporting organizations, or simply kept to help us scale our operations by targeting our dollars to the best investments.

Message Testing and Digital Asset Development

A/B advertising tests on messaging and images to determine which content resonates best with donors and voters. We should use this knowledge to develop a suite of ready to use media--ads, landing pages, and posts that can be used by us or other organizations. We should test both positive and negative ads.

Develop a Social Oriented Voterfile

This begins with the purchase and organization of commercial lists with IPs and past email addresses attached. We then match this to SOS and VAN data, ping emails to check for validity, and attempt email matches for advertising through all available social APIs. This is a long term imperative. Right off the bat, we’ll be able to provide much better targeting. In the long term, this list translate our insights about what is happening at the doors and on the ground into the digital space. It will also provide crucial data points for machine learning projects in this and future elections.

Out Of District Fundraising

Match all the processes above-- digital ads, demographic and message testing, and a social voterfile, for likely donors around the state of Colorado and beyond. This could provide crucial new funding streams for battleground races.
